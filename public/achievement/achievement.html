ACM ICPC

Students of NIT Jamshedpur(Batch 2k13) secured 7th rank in ACM ICPC REGIONAL in Kolkata Region.The Seniors were Shubham Vishwakarma , Shubham Singh , Shubhendu Shisir.

2014

Tata Crucible Hackathon

In 2018, we launched the first campus edition of Tata Crucible Hackathon, where the most innovative and brightest minds among campus students competed for the coveted prize.

The hackathon was conducted across 5 zones and the East Zone finals were hosted by NIT Jamshedpur during Ojass with over a hundred of participants.The team from NIT Jamshedpur emerged victorious and hence became the East Zone champion.

2018

Competitive Programming

The members of PCON have always excelled in the field of Competitive Programming .Be it hackerearth, hackerank or codechef, the members of club have participated in and won many contests held by different colleges.

Currently NIT Jamshedpur ranks 13 out of 5801 colleges and universities on hackerearth.

ZS Associates

ZS Associates' advanced data science team conducts yearly hackathon to attract the young undergraduate data science enthusiasts.

Through this hackathon our team members have bagged full time offers as well.

Inter NIT Coding Contest

Inter Nit Coding Contest is an yearly Mega Coding Contest for all the NITs of India hosted by NIT Bhopal.

The members of PCON have regularly outshined the other NITs in this contest and also won the last edition of the yearly extravaganza.
